TOKENIZE YOUR VISUAL CONTENT
A decentralized ledger for copyright registration of media assets
VIM is a decentralized ledger for copyright registration of media assets governed by its stakeholders
It provides a system of economic incentives and rewards for content sharing to authors and allows agencies and professionals to purchase royalty-free licenses directly from content authors without any intermediaries and with zero fees.
Key features for Authors
Decentralized public ledger of ownership records
Authors are able to register the ownership for their visual content in a decentralized tamper-proof public database and set a price for license sale
Author rewards for publishing
50% of VIM token inflation forms Author Reward Pool, which incentivizes authors for content sharing based on the reaction to this photo from VIM community
Integration with hosting services and social networks
Authors will have off-chain tools to export visual content from their accounts on Flickr, Instagram, YouTube and other services.
key features for customers
Verifiable records of license purchases
All purchases made by customers are verifiable by interested third-parties and could technically prove the fact of license purchase in courts
Purchase with 0% fees
VIM allows customers to pay directly to the author without any third-parties and with zero fees, while image and video stocks collect up to 90% fees, making the content 4-5 times more expensive.
Access to unique content form hosting services and social networks
VIM allows customers to legally use large amounts of unique visual content from social networks and image/video hostings as part of their digital campaigns, presentations, or on their websites
Access to sales history
The ledger is transparent and provides access to the information about content purchases, dates, prices and overall popularity of content items
